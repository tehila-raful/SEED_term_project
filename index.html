What Is Project Seed?

Project Seed is a community project where we welcome four energetic, creative, friendly young women from all over North America to join our community for a two week stay. They come to town and....the magic starts! Each day they will be running a different program for women and girls. Some programs will be geared especially for a specific age - there are programs for teens, middle school, college students, young professionals, moms and tots, and women of all ages.
