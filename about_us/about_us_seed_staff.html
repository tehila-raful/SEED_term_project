Meet our SEED Girls!

Tamar

Rachel

Exuding positivity and good cheer Zehnny is always up for a good challenge. With years of camping experience, Zehnny was an integral staff member in camps Nageela Midwest, Nageela Orlando and most recently Agudah Midwest. Not only a staff member in camp but in school as well, Zehnny has been Torah Academy’s Sunday School permanent substitute for quite some time and is looking forward to teaching 5th and 6th grade Judaic studies in Torah Academy this year.

With a lively personality and an award winning smile, Tamar is everyone's best friend. A native Torontonian, she is a proud staff member of Camp Agudah Toronto, this year in the capacity of sports head. Tamar is the co-owner of Ahavah Dance Studio, a by-women for-women company that holds dance classes for all ages as well as a DJ special for bat mitzvahs. Buffalo Grove is excited to welcome Tamar to their Project Seed summer team!

Rachel's positivity is like a halo around her head, with an air of confidence she relates to each person and makes them feel comfortable. Reliable and driven to do what’s right, Rachel is a trend setter. Originally from New York City, Rachel calls Queens home, but she is no stranger to the Midwest having been on staff at Camp Nageela Midwest this summer. With all the campers and staff she knows, you are bound to have mutual friends! Buffalo Grove is excited to welcome Rachel to their Project Seed summer team!

Sarah Leah

Tehila

With a sparkle in her eye and a jump in her step, Sarah Leah is adored by all. With a heart large enough to fit the world, Sarah Leah overflows with kindness. A master in relating to children she is a beloved staff member of Camp Raninu in rural Pennsylvania. With an uncanny ability to see the good in others and in every situation, Sarah Leah is like a magnet - everyone wants to be around her! Buffalo Grove is excited to welcome Sarah Leah to their Project Seed summer team!

With blond hair and blue eyes you would never know Tehila is Sefardic until she starts to speak Hebrew. With a listening ear and a million-watt smile, everyone around her feel at ease instantly. Playing a lead role in her Israel program’s musical, Tehila didn't leave a dry eye in the house! She is the supervisor of a large Brooklyn-based day camp and always has something exciting up her sleeve. As a staff member in The Zone camp for Girls, Tehila is no stranger to real good fun! Buffalo Grove is excited to welcome Tehila to their Project Seed summer team!
