About SEED Buffalo Grove

As of 2019 Buffalo Grove offers Jewish Summer programming for everyone in the community. For two weeks there is non stop fun for all ages. The programs and activities are all local, taking place at Torah Academy of BG or at the home of the Zhenwirth family. SEED also provides programs at Senior Living homes and at local day camps. To top it all off, our programmers and counselors are flown in from around the world to turn your summer into a wonderful Jewish experience!
