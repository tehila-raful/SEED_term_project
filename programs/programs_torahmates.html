Torah Mates

Learning with a partner is a big part of SEED Buffalo Grove. Once a day, week or month, whatever works for you and your partner. Together with your study partner, you learn a Jewish topic that interests you. Many of our Torah Mates are partnered with our SEED staff, It's also a great way to keep in touch!
