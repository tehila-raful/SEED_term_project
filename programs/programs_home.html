Our Programs

SEED Buffalo Grove offers many different programs for the Jewish community. Welcome teens, toddlers, Moms and more, there are programs here for everyone! Summer programs can vary between dance parties and family barbecues. Our Torah Mates program is an all year program, where members pair up and learn together throughout the year. Stay tuned for any upcoming programs!
